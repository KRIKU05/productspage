    First Public Performance at a Ram Navami Utsav

    It was during the sacred celebration of Ram Navami that Shailendra Bhartti stepped onto the stage for his very first public performance. With heartfelt devotion and a naturally soulful voice, he mesmerized the audience — marking the beginning of his journey as a devotional singer.

    Debut Devotional Album released: “Bhakti Ki Ganga”

    The release of Bhakti Ki Ganga was a major milestone in Shailendra Bhartti’s musical journey. This debut album, filled with deeply spiritual and melodious bhajans, resonated with devotees across the country.
